About Snout & Sprout

Location

Located in Harare, Snout & Sprout (S&S) is a dedicated farm specializing in raising chickens and pigs, alongside cultivating a variety of fruits and vegetables.

What we offer

We provide high-quality meat and fresh produce for wholesale and retail, ensuring timely deliveries with our motorbike service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate and deliver nutritious and diverse agricultural products through innovative and sustainable farming practices and a user-friendly online marketplace.

Vision

To be a leading provider of high-quality, sustainable agricultural products, including swine, fruits, vegetables, mushrooms and spices, through an innovative online marketplace that connects out farm to your table, promoting environmental stewardship and community well-being.

Our Management

Khulani Nkomo - Snout & Sprout Farm Manager

Our Goals

We are committed to supporting our local community and contributing to a healthier planet.

Goal #1

" Raising healthy swine with superior animal husbandry."

Goal #2

" Growing fresh fruits, vegetables, mushrooms and spices using eco-friendly methods."

Goal #3

" Ensuring the highest standards of food safety and quality."
